# Chinese Embedding and Reranker Models

Embedding, reranking, and retrieval models for RAG pipelines, with strong
Chinese-language coverage.

| Area | Notable sources | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Embeddings | BAAI (BGE family), Qwen embeddings | BGE models are widely used for Chinese retrieval. |
| Rerankers | BAAI (BGE reranker) | Cross-encoder rerankers for RAG. |

Benchmark coverage: see [C-MTEB / MTEB](https://huggingface.co/spaces/mteb/leaderboard)
for Chinese embedding evaluation.
