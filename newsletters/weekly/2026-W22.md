## New Models & Releases

**Tencent Hunyuan Hy-MT2** — Tencent's Hunyuan team published the Hy-MT2 translation model series on both GitHub and Hugging Face. Three base sizes are available: 1.8B (dense), 7B (dense), and 30B-A3B (MoE). All three target Chinese ↔ English ↔ French translation and are built on the `hunyuan_v1_dense` / `hy_v3` architectures. The paper reference is arxiv:2512.24092.
[GitHub](https://github.com/Tencent-Hunyuan/Hy-MT2) · [HF 1.8B](https://huggingface.co/tencent/Hy-MT2-1.8B) · [HF 7B](https://huggingface.co/tencent/Hy-MT2-7B) · [HF 30B-A3B](https://huggingface.co/tencent/Hy-MT2-30B-A3B)

**OpenBMB MiniCPM-V 4.6 Thinking — quantized variants** — OpenBMB released AWQ, BNB, GPTQ, and GGUF quantized builds of MiniCPM-V-4.6-Thinking, complementing the base model. The lightweight multimodal model (arxiv:2604.27393) is designed for on-device inference.
[HF AWQ](https://huggingface.co/openbmb/MiniCPM-V-4.6-Thinking-AWQ) · [HF GGUF](https://huggingface.co/openbmb/MiniCPM-V-4.6-Thinking-gguf) · [HF GPTQ](https://huggingface.co/openbmb/MiniCPM-V-4.6-Thinking-GPTQ) · [HF BNB](https://huggingface.co/openbmb/MiniCPM-V-4.6-Thinking-BNB)
