## New Models & Releases

**Tencent Hunyuan Hy3** — Tencent Hunyuan published **[Hy3](https://github.com/Tencent-Hunyuan/Hy3)** alongside Hugging Face checkpoints for **[tencent/Hy3](https://huggingface.co/tencent/Hy3)** and **[tencent/Hy3-FP8](https://huggingface.co/tencent/Hy3-FP8)**. The repository describes Hy3 as a **295B total / 21B active** Mixture-of-Experts model for reasoning and agent use cases, with an emphasis on cost efficiency. The FP8 variant should make the release more practical for lower-cost serving experiments.

**Tencent HunyuanOCR** — Tencent published **[HunyuanOCR](https://huggingface.co/tencent/HunyuanOCR)** on Hugging Face, tagged for OCR, vision-language modeling, document parsing, and text spotting. The model card places it in the `hunyuan_vl` family and frames it as an image-text-to-text model, making it relevant for document AI workflows rather than only generic visual QA.

**Tencent Ex-Omni** — Tencent also updated **[Ex-Omni](https://huggingface.co/tencent/Ex-Omni)**, a safetensors checkpoint tagged with `qwen3` and arxiv:2602.07106. Details are sparse in the rollup, but the release adds another omni-modal model artifact to Tencent's public Hugging Face footprint.

**OpenBMB MiniCPM-o-4.5** — OpenBMB published **[MiniCPM-o-4.5](https://huggingface.co/openbmb/MiniCPM-o-4_5)**, tagged with `minicpmo`, `minicpm-o`, `minicpm-v`, multimodal, ONNX, and feature-extraction metadata. This extends the MiniCPM multimodal/on-device family with another practical deployment-oriented artifact.

## Robotics, Multimodal & Research

**Qwen-RobotNav** — Qwen opened **[Qwen-RobotNav](https://github.com/QwenLM/Qwen-RobotNav)**, described as the official repository for Qwen-RobotNav. Coming shortly after Qwen-AgentWorld and Tencent HY-Embodied, this keeps embodied navigation and robotics visibly active in the Chinese open model ecosystem.

**Tencent Hunyuan MixGRPO** — Tencent Hunyuan updated **[MixGRPO](https://github.com/Tencent-Hunyuan/MixGRPO)**, the ECCV 2026 project “MixGRPO: Unlocking Flow-based GRPO Efficiency with Mixed ODE-SDE.” The work sits at the intersection of reinforcement learning and flow-based generation, and is worth tracking for teams following GRPO-style post-training beyond text-only LLMs.

**Tencent Hunyuan GEAR** — Tencent Hunyuan also published or updated **[GEAR](https://github.com/Tencent-Hunyuan/GEAR)**. The draft has limited metadata, but the repository is now tracked from the official Hunyuan organization.

**Tencent Hunyuan Rosetta-inference** — **[Rosetta-inference](https://github.com/Tencent-Hunyuan/Rosetta-inference)** surfaced with the description “Open-source native multimodal pretraining — without catastrophic forgetting.” That positioning makes it especially relevant for multimodal continual pretraining and inference workflows.
